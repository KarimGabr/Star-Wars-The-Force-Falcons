Welcome to Star Wars: The Force Falcons

After Breaking the Great Wall of the Dark Side, our star fighters are engaged to destroy all enemy ships.

Game Instructions

The Online Game can take a while loading, also may encounter some lag at the beginning.

  • Press "Space" and "KeypadNum0" to change direction of the space ships
  • Touch the left and the right side of the screen to change direction of the space ships

Unity 3D Game Engine

The Game is developed with Unity and MonoDevelop with C# as scripting Language. Unity 5.4 now features exporting to ALL platforms.
